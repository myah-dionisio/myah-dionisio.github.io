ABOUT ME

I am deeply passionate about fashion, art, and design. After finishing high school, I completed a Bachelor of Fashion Design, where I developed strong technical skills in sewing and patternmaking. These experiences have become the foundation of my teaching practice as a Design and Technologies teacher, allowing me to integrate creative and practical processes into the classroom.

I am continually expanding my expertise across other areas of Design and Technologies, including metalwork and timber, as I believe in modelling lifelong learning for my students. A true lover of learning, I draw inspiration from the world around me - from the textures, forms, and ideas that shape our everyday lives - and channel this into my teaching and creative work.
