My Background

My name is Myah Dionisio and I am a high school teacher at The Hills Grammar School. I am currently still studying at Australian Catholic University as a fourth year student.

Teaching Areas

My teaching areas are Design & Technologies, Textiles & Design, and Visual Arts.

I have a very deep interest in fashion and have studied Bachelor of Fashion Design after high school. With the skills learnt from previous studies, I have been able to integrate them into my teaching practice as a D&T/TAS teacher. I am still developing my skills in other areas of D&T, including metalwork and timber.
